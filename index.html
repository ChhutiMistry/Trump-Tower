Sector 65, Gurgaon

Tribeca Trump Tower is a residential project by Tribeca Creators LLP. Located at Sector 65 in Gurgaon, this posh high-rise comprises spacious 3 BHK and 4 BHK apartments. The unit size ranges from 3,500 Sq.Ft. to 6,000 Sq.Ft. .As you enter Tribeca Trump Tower, you will get lured by the signature glass facade. Residents can enjoy in a temperature-controlled indoor pool. Get rejuvenated in the spa and fitness centre with sauna, steam. Enjoy a relaxing massage in a massage room.
